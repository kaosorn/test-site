At our events, our wedding DJs job is to make sure that your guests are getting up out of their seats getting down on the dance floor!!

Your wedding should be one of the best memories of your life. Get Up to Get Down is experienced in making that memory a lifetime favorite. Choose a top wedding DJ professional. Our secret to success is in the “details” of your wedding. We meet with our couples on two different occasions. One, this allows us to get to know the two of you. Two, we will go over wedding etiquette, song choices, agenda, and Up-Lighting colors etc. Three, we will collaborate on your timeline or the flow of your day.
